Diversity, Equity & Inclusion

The "Figure It Out" Framework for Accessibility

True inclusion isn't just about meeting ADA requirements. It is about fostering a culture of adaptation. Xavier Sanchez learned this at age two at Respite Care of San Antonio, where the philosophy was simple: if a space doesn't work for you, we figure it out together. He later applied this at the Believe It Foundation, where "bridging the gap" meant ensuring every camper, regardless of ability, took the same risks on the zip line.

Xavier moves the DEI conversation from "awareness" to "actionable advocacy." He shares how removing the social awkwardness around disability leads to stronger, more honest, and more innovative teams.
