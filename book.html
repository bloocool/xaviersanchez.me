Community, Faith & Nonprofits

“Born Twice”: Overcoming, Belonging, and the Power of Inclusion

I was born without arms and with partial legs into a world that wasn’t built for me — and from day one, the people around me refused to accept that as the end of the story.

This talk traces my whole journey: growing up in mainstream classrooms when that wasn’t the norm, learning what it feels like to be stared at and what it feels like to truly belong, finding communities that said “we’ll figure it out together” instead of pointing to a policy. It’s about what happens when you stop waiting for the world to accommodate you and start moving forward anyway — and what it means to build spaces where nobody gets left behind.

The message isn’t just about disability. It’s about anyone who has ever felt like they don’t fit the mold — and choosing to show up anyway.

Best for: youth programs, faith communities, nonprofits, correctional facilities, recovery programs, and schools.

Corporate & Leadership Events

“Bend the Rules, Figure It Out, and Solve for X”

My whole life, the standard playbook didn’t apply to me. So I learned early to bend the rules, figure it out, and find a solution — not because I’m exceptional, but because I had no other choice.

That mindset followed me into my career. I’ve worked in sales, gotten experience in fintech operations at Revolut, and built real relationships managing clients at Cruising Kitchens. None of those roles had a manual for someone like me. What they had were problems that needed solving — and I’ve never met a problem that didn’t have a way through.

This keynote is about taking that same approach and applying it to your team: how to stay adaptable when conditions change, how to lead without a perfect plan, and how solving for X — whatever your X is — starts with refusing to accept that it can’t be done.

Best for: corporate off-sites, leadership summits, sales teams, and organizations navigating change.
